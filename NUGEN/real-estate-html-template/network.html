Lease line

To secure a leased line, your best approach is to contact telecom providers or internet service providers (ISPs) that offer this service in your area. Begin by researching online for ISPs or telecom companies that specialize in leased line solutions. Local directories, business listings, and technology service directories can provide valuable leads.

Additionally, reaching out to other businesses in your area that utilize leased lines for recommendations can be beneficial. Once you compile a list of potential providers, contact them directly to request quotes.

MPLS

MPLS (Multiprotocol Label Switching) represents a sophisticated solution for businesses seeking to optimize their network infrastructure. At NUGEN COMMUNICATION Pvt Ltd, we offer MPLS services designed to enhance the efficiency, security, and reliability of your corporate network.

MPLS enables prioritized traffic routing through virtual private networks (VPNs), ensuring seamless communication between multiple locations while maintaining stringent quality of service (QoS) standards. Our MPLS solutions provide a robust framework for secure data transmission, incorporating advanced encryption and traffic segregation to safeguard sensitive information.

P2P / NLD

One of the most highly-touted benefits of using P2P technology lies in its ability to cut bandwidth bills. Simply put, infrastructure costs a lot of money to set up, so it’s much better when individual users take on the hosting and distribution load instead of relying on traditional servers.

A good example of a major company that couldn’t exist without P2P is Skype, the famed online phone business that allows you to call your friends and family for free over the Internet.

SD WAN

D-WAN architecture offering on-site equipment that connects to a cloud gateway (or virtual gateway). This provides the benefits of an on-premise SD-WAN (real time traffic shaping for example) with the added benefits of performance boosts when companies use cloud applications. This is most beneficial in situations where connectivity becomes an issue,

but cloud applications can remain connected to a gateway that’s networked directly to a cloud provider. Those gateways can keep sessions active for cloud applications. It’s common for cloud apps to run over public internet where they’re controlled by the company’s SD-WAN

Mix Bandthwidth

Mixed bandwidth combines multiple internet connections or technologies to achieve faster speeds and reliable networking. By aggregating bandwidth, businesses can optimize data transfer, ensure network redundancy, and improve quality of service. SD-WAN and NFV technologies help allocate resources efficiently. Mixed bandwidth solutions offer flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness.

They are ideal for organizations with high network demands, such as cloud-based applications, video streaming, and online backup. By leveraging mixed bandwidth, businesses can maximize their network's potential, enhance productivity, and stay ahead in a competitive market.

Broadband

Broadband is a high-speed internet connection providing fast and reliable access to online content. With speeds exceeding 25 Mbps, broadband enables seamless streaming, online gaming, and heavy internet usage. Technologies like DSL, cable modem, fiber-optic, and mobile broadband offer always-on connectivity. Broadband has transformed the way we communicate, work, and entertain ourselves, facilitating video conferencing, cloud computing, and online collaboration.

Its impact on education, business, and healthcare has been significant, making it an essential utility in today's digital age. Broadband's high speed and reliability have made it an indispensable part of modern life.
